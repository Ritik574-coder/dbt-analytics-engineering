# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming space for data engineers and contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus criticism on ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Repeated disruptive or bad-faith interactions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it through the security/contact process in `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code.
